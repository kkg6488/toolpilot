Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Weekend binge eating — one cheat day erases 4–5 days of deficit
  2. Cutting calories below 1,500 — you'll lose muscle and crash metabolically
  3. Skipping protein — missing 150g even a few days/week = muscle loss
  4. Excessive cardio (1+ hr daily) instead of weight training
  5. Poor sleep (under 7 hours) — tanks growth hormone and testosterone
  6. Daily weigh-ins and panicking — weigh weekly, same conditions
  7. Program hopping — stick to this plan for minimum 12 weeks before changing
  8. Ignoring back and legs because you can't see them in the mirror

Calorie Adjustment Protocol

Every 4 weeks, assess:
• If weight loss stalls 2 weeks straight → reduce calories by 100 (cut 25g carbs)
• If strength drops significantly → add 100 kcal back (add 25g carbs)
• Add 5 min to post-workout walks BEFORE dropping calories
• Never go below 1,500 kcal — if you stall at 1,500, it's a NEAT/cardio problem
