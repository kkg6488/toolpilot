Why 2,000 kcal Failed

At 2,000 kcal, your theoretical deficit was ~827 kcal/day. But you were NOT actually eating 2,000. Unmeasured cooking oil (1 tbsp extra = +120 kcal), ghee on roti (+45 kcal), an extra helping of rice (+130 kcal), chai with sugar (+70 kcal). These invisible calories added 300–500 kcal/day. Your REAL intake was probably 2,300–2,500 kcal — essentially maintenance. That's why the scale didn't move.

Family Cooking Instructions (Print This)

Rules for ALL Meals — Give This to Whoever Cooks
  • MAX 1 teaspoon (5ml) oil per dish. Not 1 tablespoon. Use a measuring spoon. 1 extra tbsp = +120 invisible calories.
  • NO ghee on roti. Dry roti only. Every time. No exceptions. Ghee on 2 rotis = +90 kcal you didn't plan for.
  • NO sugar in anything. No chai with sugar, no sweetened curd, no sugar in dal.
  • Weigh chicken AFTER cooking — it shrinks ~25%. 180g cooked = ~240g raw. Don't confuse the two.
  • Weigh rice AFTER cooking. 100g cooked rice = ~40g dry rice. Cook measured amounts.
  • Boil or steam vegetables when possible. If making sabzi, use MAX 1 tsp oil for the whole thing.
  • Soy chunks: Soak 40g dry in hot water 15 min, squeeze out water completely, then cook in curry with 1 tsp oil.
  • Dal should be thick, not watery. More protein per serving when thick.
  • Roti: Use 30g dry atta per roti. No extra flour dusting. No butter/ghee on top.

10 Mistakes That Will Destroy Progress

  1. 01
    Not Weighing Food

    You think 2,000 kcal but eat 2,800. Buy a ₹500 kitchen scale. Weigh everything for the first month.

  2. 02
    Unmeasured Cooking Oil

    1 extra tablespoon of oil = +120 kcal. Over a week = +840 kcal = 0 fat loss. Use measuring spoons.

  3. 03
    Skipping Protein on Veg Days

    Dal has only 7–9g protein per serving. You need 160g. Whey + soy chunks are mandatory.

  4. 04
    Too Much Cardio, Not Enough Lifting

    Running 5km while skipping weights = skinny-fat. Weights preserve muscle. Cardio is supplementary.

  5. 05
    "Cheat Day" Every Weekend

    One binge = 3,000+ extra calories. Erases the entire week. No cheat days. One cheat MEAL every 2 weeks max.

  6. 06
    Program Hopping

    Changing workouts every 2 weeks from YouTube. Stick to THIS for 12 weeks minimum.

  7. 07
    Sleeping 5–6 Hours

    Increases cortisol (muscle wasting), ghrelin (hunger), decreases testosterone. 7+ hours or you sabotage everything.

  8. 08
    Obsessing Over Daily Scale Weight

    Weight fluctuates 1–2 kg daily from water/sodium. Weigh weekly average. Use waist measurement as primary indicator.

  9. 09
    Skipping Gym on Office Days

    "Too tired" is not a reason. Train at 8:30 PM after office. You have the time.

  10. 10
    Ghee on Roti / Sugar in Chai

    Ghee on 2 rotis = +90 kcal. Chai with sugar 3x/day = +210 kcal. These "small" things = 0 fat loss.
